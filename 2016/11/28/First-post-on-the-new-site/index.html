Discovered Hexo

… and a few hours of frantic finger-mashing on the keyboard + rearding the documentation and we have a web page again! Thanks Hexo!!

Truth be told this framework has been on the “hitlist” to check out for a while now and I’m glad I did. Apart from running into the odd documentation bits here and there in languages I could not understand, I have really enjoyed the process of learning to use it.

Could not have been done without:

  • Good documentation, thanks Hexo Contributors!
  • Lot’s of coffee,
  • Great sounds (like Martyrdöd, Brainoil, High on Fire and Pallbearer) on the headphones, thanks!

Still, lots of work left to do so no time dillydally …
