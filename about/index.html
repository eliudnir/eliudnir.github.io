About eliudnir

Hel’s hall, sprayed with snowstorms

Now:

  • Age: somewhere between ‘not young‘ and ‘oh wow, that doesn’t sound right?!‘
  • Resident: San Francisco, CA since ‘08 with a healthy burrito addiction ever since
  • Digits: still have them all
  • Drives: on the right side of the road
  • Cooks: almost anything except meth and smalahove
  • Doctrine: arch

Before:

eliudnir cultivated slowly in an estuary mixing out of the cold feh-yords (gesundheit) of a small Scandinavian country. There he studied music, programming and eventually acquired sentience and full motor-control. Having tired of palavering in his native tongue for many years, he decided it was time to move the sleeping bag & coffee mug to a new location.

The next five years was spent in the great city of London working on mobile and smartphone games, learning what pubs are good for, and figuring out that Sweden was in fact not a made-up place! After a quick move to the bay area and several more years spent working on games and smartphone related stuff, he got married at SF City Hall in ‘09.

Distractions:

Guitar:

I play as often as I can and have been doing so since I was about 8 years old. This goes beyond addiction and I get geeky about anything from wood grains to neck profiles, tube amplifiers and on and on, basically everything guitar!

Snowboarding:

Hailing originally from “DatOdderSnowmound, Scandicold” I’m no stranger to playing in the snow, and yes, I would like to carpool with you to Tahoe!

Games:

Having worked with games for a while there’s no shortage of distractions in this category either, I basically like most games. If I had to mention just one game though, the one that sticks out to me personally throughout the years would be Midnight Sun 2, a Multi User Dungeon game over telnet (rpg/fantasy). Nothing can beat that old text nostalgia, hah …

Mountain Biking:

Anyone that rides is probably thinking “well yeah that figures, he lives on the west coast”. And yeah, we got pretty epic mountain biking terrain and access out here.
